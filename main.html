About the Game

Abyssal Realms is a survival horror game set in a nightmarish open world. Players must navigate through dark, haunting environments filled with terrifying creatures that could emerge at any moment. The game’s world is randomly generated, and every corner holds new horrors for those brave enough to explore it.

The game is designed to be an unforgiving experience: players will need to survive, gather resources, and fight monsters to earn valuable loot. However, death is not the end – dying will reset your progress, but the fear of losing everything pushes you to become stronger and more cunning with each attempt.

The world is filled with randomly spawning entities, from terrifying low-tier monsters to rare, mythical creatures that will challenge even the most seasoned hunters. These entities drop valuable items that can be used for crafting or can be shown off to flex your survival skills to others.

Survive, fight, collect, and conquer. The Abyss watches every move you make.

Rare Entities

The Spectral Wraith

Spawned only under the cover of the darkest nights, the Spectral Wraith is a ghostly entity that glides silently across the land. Players who encounter this being must solve cryptic puzzles to banish it, or face an agonizing death.

The Abyssal Beast

A beast of horrifying size and strength, the Abyssal Beast has the ability to tear through anything in its path. This terrifying creature has a 1% spawn chance, and defeating it unlocks the Abyssal Blade, a weapon capable of slaying even the toughest foes.

The Bloodthirsty Phantom

Born from the cursed ground, the Bloodthirsty Phantom appears in desolate, blood-soaked locations. Known for its life-draining abilities, this entity is as rare as it is dangerous, with a 0.5% spawn rate.
